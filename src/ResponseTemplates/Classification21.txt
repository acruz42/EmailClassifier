Q: "What is the requirement on my English (e.g., TOEFL/IELTS) scores? Can I waive my TOEFL/IELTS exams?"

A: Please find the requirement on English exams from <a href="https://isss.nmsu.edu/index-8/">https://isss.nmsu.edu/index-8/</a>. Note that students from some countries can have their TOEFL/IELTS exams waived. You can find the list of such countries from <a href="https://isss.nmsu.edu/index-8/">https://isss.nmsu.edu/index-8/</a>.

NOTE: For U.S. citizens there is no English language test requirement
